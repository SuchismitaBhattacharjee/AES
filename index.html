Society at Galance

Founded in 1935 under the visionary guidance of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, the Ahmedabad Education Society (AES) stands as one of Gujarat’s most enduring symbols of educational excellence and civic pride. What began as a patriotic mission to create institutions that fostered freedom of thought has today grown into a vibrant academic ecosystem shaping thousands of young minds every year.

Spanning over 700 acres in the heart of Ahmedabad, AES is home to a distinguished cluster of colleges and research institutions across diverse disciplines — Arts, Commerce, Science, Pharmacy, Architecture, Planning, Management, and Technology.

Guided by the ideals of its founders — G. V. Mavlankar, Sheth Kasturbhai Lalbhai, Anandshanker Dhruv, Amritlal Hargovandas, and Balvantrai Thakore — the Society continues to blend heritage with innovation, ensuring education remains both relevant and rooted.

Chairman’s Message

Mr. Sanjay Lalbhai is the chairman and managing director, Arvind Limited,(formerly the Arvind Mills Ltd.) a large integrated textile mill, a company presiding over the businesses of textiles, clothing, and infrastructure development. He is presiding over the operations of all the textile and clothing activities of the Lalbhai Group. Mr. Lalbhai is on the Board of Governors of the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad. He is also the president of Ahmedabad Education Society, presiding over a number of well known educational institutions, in Gujarat. Mr. Lalbhai is the chairman of ATIRA's (Ahmedabad Textile Industry’s Research Association) Council of Administration.
